Matthew 12

SET #1

S 1. Situation question: who said it, to whom, and in reply to what: "If any of you has a sheep and it falls into a pit on the Sabbath, will you not take hold of it and lift it out?"
A. Jesus said it to the Pharisees, in reply to, "Is it lawful to heal on the Sabbath?" (Matthew 12:10-11)

A 2. According to Matthew, chapter 12, verse 20, what will he not break?
A. A bruised reed (Matthew 12:20)

V 3. Finish this verse: "For the Son of Man. . ."
A. ". . .is Lord of the Sabbath." (Matthew 12:8)

G 4. Who will be given the sign of the prophet Jonah?
A. A wicked and adulterous generation (Matthew 12:39)

G 5. Who will proclaim justice to the nations?
A. My servant (Matthew 12:18)

A 6. According to Matthew, chapter 12, verse 31, what can be forgiven?
A. Every kind of sin and slander (Matthew 12:31)

G 7. Who will be three days and three nights in the heart of the earth?
A. The Son of Man (Matthew 12:40)

R 8. Finish this verse and give the reference: "For the Son of Man. . ."
A. ". . .is Lord of the Sabbath." (Matthew 12:8)

G 9. To whom did the Pharisees say, "Look! Your disciples are doing what is unlawful on the Sabbath."?
A. Jesus (Matthew 12:2)

X 10. In Matthew, chapter 12, of what was Jesus aware?
A. That the Pharisees were plotting how they might kill him (Matthew 12:14-15)

G 11. Why will the Queen of the South rise at the judgment with this generation and condemn it?
A. For she came from the ends of the earth to listen to Solomon's wisdom, and now something greater than Solomon is here (Matthew 12:42)

G 12. Who said, "Could this be the Son of David?"
A. All the people (Matthew 12:23)

Q 13. Quote Matthew, chapter 12, verse 8.
A. "For the Son of Man is Lord of the Sabbath."" (Matthew 12:8)

G 14. What will anyone who speaks a word against the Son of Man be?
A. Forgiven (Matthew 12:32)

A 15. According to Matthew, chapter 12, verse 24, who heard this?
A. The Pharisees (Matthew 12:24)

G 16. When will everyone have to give account for every empty word they have spoken?
A. On the day of judgment (Matthew 12:36)

G 17. Than what is the final condition of that person worse?
A. The first (condition) (Matthew 12:45)

A 18. According to Matthew, chapter 12, verse 27, what will they be?
A. Your judges (Matthew 12:27)

G 19. What does the mouth speak?
A. What the heart is full of (Matthew 12:34)

G 20. When does an impure spirit say, "I will return to the house I left."?
A. When it comes out of a person and goes through arid places seeking rest and does not find it (Matthew 12:43-44)
