A. To leave their region (Matthew 8:34)

A. Jesus said it to those following him (Matthew 8:10)

A. The demons (Matthew 8:31)

A. ". . . and bore our diseases." (Matthew 8:17)

A. His disciples (Matthew 8:23)

A. Those tending the pigs (Matthew 8:33)

V 17. Finish this verse: "He replied, "You of little. . ."
A. ". . .faith, why are you so afraid?" Then he got up and rebuked the winds and the waves, and it was completely calm." (Matthew 8:26)
