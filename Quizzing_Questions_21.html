SET #1

G 1. To whom did Jesus say, "Go to the village ahead of you, and at once you will find a donkey tied there, with her colt by her. Untie them and bring them to me."?
A. Two disciples (Matthew 21:1-2)

G 2. Who said, "They will respect my son."?
A. A landowner (Matthew 21:37)

Q 3. Quote Matthew, chapter 21, verse 21.
A. "Jesus replied, "Truly I tell you, if you have faith and do not doubt, not only can you do what was done to the fig tree, but also you can say to this mountain, 'Go, throw yourself into the sea,' and it will be done." (Matthew 21:21)

G 4. Why did the disciples place their cloaks on the donkey and the colt?
A. For Jesus to sit on (Matthew 21:7)

A 5. According to Matthew, chapter 21, verse 3, what should you say?
A. That the Lord needs them (Matthew 21:3)

G 6. What if the chief priests and the elders of the people say, "From heaven."?
A. Jesus will ask, "Then why didn't you believe him?" (Matthew 21:25)

S 7. Situation question: who said it, to whom, and where: "By what authority are you doing these things? And who gave you this authority?"
A. The chief priests and the elders of the people said it to Jesus, in the temple courts (Matthew 21:23)

G 8. Whom did the tax collectors and the prostitutes believe?
A. John (Matthew 21:32)

G 9. Who was hungry early in the morning as he was on his way back to the city?
A. Jesus (Matthew 21:18)

X 10. Complete, in essence, the following Old Testament quotation: "From the lips of children . . ."
A. ". . . and infants you, Lord, have called forth your praise." (Matthew 21:16)

A 11. According to Matthew, chapter 21, verse 28, who was there?
A. A man who had two sons (Matthew 21:28)

G 12. When will the owner of the vineyard bring those wretches to a wretched end?
A. When the owner of the vineyard comes (Matthew 21:40-41)

V 13. Finish this verse: "The crowds that went ahead. . ."
A. ". . .of him and those that followed shouted, "Hosanna to the Son of David!" "Blessed is he who comes in the name of the Lord!" "Hosanna in the highest heaven!" (Matthew 21:9)

A 14. According to Matthew, chapter 21, verse 2, what will they at once find?
A. A donkey tied in the village ahead of them, with her colt by her (Matthew 21:2)

G 15. Who comes to Daughter Zion, gentle and riding on a donkey, on a colt, the foal of a donkey?
A. Your king (Matthew 21:5)

G 16. Who built a watchtower?
A. A landowner (Matthew 21:33)

A 17. According to Matthew, chapter 21, verse 44, who will be crushed?
A. Anyone on whom this stone falls (Matthew 21:44)

G 18. When did a landowner send his servants to the tenants to collect his fruit?
A. When the harvest time approached (Matthew 21:34)

R 19. Finish this verse and give the reference: "The crowds that went ahead. . ."
A. ". . .of him and those that followed shouted, "Hosanna to the Son of David!" "Blessed is he who comes in the name of the Lord!" "Hosanna in the highest heaven!" " (Matthew 21:9)

G 20. What has the stone the builders rejected become?
