SET #1

G 1. Who will not taste death before they see the Son of Man coming in his kingdom?
A. Some who are standing here (Matthew 16:28)

V 2. Finish these verses: "Then Jesus said to his. . ."
A. ". . .disciples, "Whoever wants to be my disciple must deny themselves and take up their cross and follow me. For whoever wants to save their life will lose it, but whoever loses their life for me will find it." (Matthew 16:24-25)

A 3. According to Matthew, chapter 16, verse 1, how did the Pharisees and Sadducees test Jesus?
A. By asking him to show them a sign from heaven (Matthew 16:1)

G 4. What did the disciples forget to take when they went across the lake?
A. Bread (Matthew 16:5)

G 5. What will the gates of Hades not overcome?
A. Jesus' church (Matthew 16:18)

Q 6. Quote Matthew, chapter 16, verses 17 through 18.
A. "Jesus replied, "Blessed are you, Simon son of Jonah, for this was not revealed to you by flesh and blood, but by my Father in heaven. And I tell you that you are Peter, and on this rock I will build my church, and the gates of Hades will not overcome it." (Matthew 16:17-18)

X 7. When will it be fair weather?
A. When evening comes and the sky is red (Matthew 16:2)

A 8. According to Matthew, chapter 16, verse 12, against what should they guard?
A. The teaching of the Pharisees and Sadducees (Matthew 16:12)

G 9. Who are talking among themselves about having no bread?
A. The disciples (Matthew 16:8)

G 10. With whom is the Son of Man going to come in his Father's glory?
A. His angels (Matthew 16:27)

R 11. Finish this verse and give the reference: "From that time on Jesus. . ."
A. ". . .began to explain to his disciples that he must go to Jerusalem and suffer many things at the hands of the elders, the chief priests and the teachers of the law, and that he must be killed and on the third day be raised to life. " (Matthew 16:21)

G 12. What do the Pharisees and Sadducees say in the morning?
A. "Today it will be stormy, for the sky is red and overcast." (Matthew 16:3)

G 13. What does Peter have in mind?
A. Merely human concerns (Matthew 16:23)

S 14. Situation question: who said it, and in reply to what: "Blessed are you, Simon son of Jonah, for this was not revealed to you by flesh and blood, but by my Father in heaven."?
A. Jesus said it in reply to, "You are the Messiah, the Son of the living God." (Matthew 16:16-17)

A 15. According to Matthew, chapter 16, verse 17, who is blessed?
A. Simon son of Jonah (Matthew 16:17)

G 16. Whom did Peter begin to rebuke?
A. Jesus (Matthew 16:22)

A 17. According to Matthew, chapter 16, verse 19, what will be bound in heaven?
A. Whatever you bind on earth (Matthew 16:19)

G 18. Who said, "It is because we didn't bring any bread."?
A. The disciples (Matthew 16:7)

G 19. What will whoever loses their life for Jesus find?
A. Their life (Matthew 16:25)

G 20. What must whoever wants to be Jesus' disciple do?
A. Deny themselves and take up their cross and follow Jesus (Matthew 16:24)
