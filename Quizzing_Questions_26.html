A. Peter (Matthew 26:69)

A. Those standing there (Matthew 26:73)

A. From now on (Matthew 26:64)

A. Judas (Matthew 26:49)

A. A hymn (Matthew 26:30)

A. The chief priests and the elders of the people (Matthew 26:4)

A. Jesus (Matthew 26:50)

A. ". . .took bread, and when he had given thanks, he broke it and gave it to his disciples, saying, "Take and eat; this is my body." (Matthew 26:26)

A. The disciples said it to Jesus, and the reply was, "Go into the city to a certain man and tell him, 'The Teacher says: My appointed time is near. I am going to celebrate the Passover with my disciples at your house.'" (Matthew 26:17-18)

A. My blood of the covenant (which is poured out for many for the forgiveness of sins) (Matthew 26:28)

A. That man who betrays the Son of Man (Matthew 26:24)

A. Many (Matthew 26:28)

G 10. Whom did Jesus ask, "Couldn't you men keep watch with me for one hour?"
A. Peter (Matthew 26:40)

A 11. According to Matthew, chapter 26, verse 18, what should you tell a certain man?
A. "The Teacher says: My appointed time is near. I am going to celebrate the Passover with my disciples at your house." (Matthew 26:18)

G 12. With what did Peter deny it again?
A. An oath (Matthew 26:72)

G 13. Why did Peter sit down with the guards?
A. To see the outcome (Matthew 26:58)

R 14. Finish this verse and give the reference: "While they were eating, Jesus. . ."
A. ". . .took bread, and when he had given thanks, he broke it and gave it to his disciples, saying, "Take and eat; this is my body." " (Matthew 26:26)

A 15. According to Matthew, chapter 26, verse 17, when did the disciples come to Jesus?
A. On the first day of the Festival of Unleavened Bread (Matthew 26:17)

G 16. Who said, "But not during the festival, or there may be a riot among the people."?
A. The chief priests and the elders of the people (Matthew 26:5)

G 17. Whom did the men arrest?
A. Jesus (Matthew 26:50)

V 18. Finish this verse: "Going a little farther, he. . ."
A. ". . .fell with his face to the ground and prayed, "My Father, if it is possible, may this cup be taken from me. Yet not as I will, but as you will." (Matthew 26:39)

G 19. Whom did others slap?
A. Jesus (Matthew 26:67)

G 20. Where did Jesus and his disciples go when they had sung a hymn?
A. Out to the Mount of Olives (Matthew 26:30)
