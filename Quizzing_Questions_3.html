Matthew 3

SET #1

G 1. Who saw the Spirit of God descending like a dove and alighting on him?
A. Jesus (Matthew 3:16)

A 2. According to Matthew, chapter 3, verse 15, what did Jesus reply?
A. "Let it be so now; it is proper for us to do this to fulfill all righteousness" (Matthew 3:15)

G 3. Whose food was locusts and wild honey?
A. John's (the Baptist) (Matthew 3:4)

Q 4. Quote Matthew, chapter 3, verses 16 through 17.
A. "As soon as Jesus was baptized, he went up out of the water. At that moment heaven was opened, and he saw the Spirit of God descending like a dove and alighting on him. And a voice from heaven said, "This is my Son, whom I love; with him I am well pleased."" (Matthew 3:16-17)

G 5. For whom can God raise up children out of these stones?
A. Abraham (Matthew 3:9)

G 6. At what is the ax already?
A. The root of the trees (Matthew 3:10)

R 7. Finish these verses and give the reference: "In those days John the. . ."
A. ". . .Baptist came, preaching in the wilderness of Judea and saying, "Repent, for the kingdom of heaven has come near." " (Matthew 3:1-2)

G 8. Where did John have a leather belt?
A. Around his waist (Matthew 3:4)

A 9. According to Matthew, chapter 3, verse 14, what did John say?
A. "I need to be baptized by you, and do you come to me?" (Matthew 3:14)

X 10. Complete, in essence, the following: "His winnowing fork is in . . ."
A. ". . . his hand, and he will clear his threshing floor, gathering his wheat into the barn and burning up the chaff with unquenchable fire." (Matthew 3:12)

G 11. What should you produce in keeping with repentance?
A. Fruit (Matthew 3:8)

A 12. According to Matthew, chapter 3, verse 3, who is this?
A. He who was spoken of through the prophet Isaiah (Matthew 3:3)

G 13. When did John the Baptist come, preaching in the wilderness of Judea?
A. In those days (Matthew 3:1)

G 14. Who said to many of the Pharisees and Sadducees: "You brood of vipers! Who warned you to flee from the coming wrath?"?
A. John (the Baptist) (Matthew 3:7)

A 15. According to Matthew, chapter 3, verse 5, who went out to him?
A. People (from Jerusalem and all Judea and the whole region of the Jordan) (Matthew 3:5)

V 16. Finish this verse: ""I baptize you with water. . ."
A. ". . .for repentance. But after me comes one who is more powerful than I, whose sandals I am not worthy to carry. He will baptize you with the Holy Spirit and fire." (Matthew 3:11)

G 17. From where did Jesus come to be baptized by John?
A. Galilee (Matthew 3:13)

G 18. Who can raise up children for Abraham out of these stones?
A. God (Matthew 3:9)

S 19. Situation question: who said it, and to whom: "Produce fruit in keeping with repentance."?
A. John (the Baptist) said it to many of the Pharisees and Sadducees (who were coming to where John was baptizing) (Matthew 3:8)

G 20. What were people confessing?
A. Their sins (Matthew 3:6)

A. John (the Baptist) said it to Jesus, when Jesus came from Galilee to the Jordan to be baptized by John (Matthew 3:13-14)

G 7. What were people confessing?
A. Their sins (Matthew 3:6)

A 8. According to Matthew, chapter 3, verse 7, whom did he see coming to where he was baptizing?
A. Many of the Pharisees and Sadducees (Matthew 3:7)

G 9. What should you produce in keeping with repentance?
A. Fruit (Matthew 3:8)

V 10. Finish these verses: "In those days John the. . ."
A. ". . .Baptist came, preaching in the wilderness of Judea and saying, "Repent, for the kingdom of heaven has come near." (Matthew 3:1-2)

G 11. What will be cut down and thrown into the fire?
A. Every tree that does not produce good fruit (Matthew 3:10)

G 12. For whom is it proper to do this to fulfill all righteousness?
A. Jesus and John (the Baptist) (Matthew 3:15)

Q 13. Quote Matthew, chapter 3, verses 16 through 17.
A. "As soon as Jesus was baptized, he went up out of the water. At that moment heaven was opened, and he saw the Spirit of God descending like a dove and alighting on him. And a voice from heaven said, "This is my Son, whom I love; with him I am well pleased."" (Matthew 3:16-17)

G 14. With what will he burn up the chaff?
A. Unquenchable fire (Matthew 3:12)

G 15. How did John try to deter Jesus?
A. Saying, "I need to be baptized by you, and do you come to me?" (Matthew 3:14)

R 16. Finish these verses and give the reference: "Produce fruit in keeping with. . ."
A. ". . .repentance. And do not think you can say to yourselves, 'We have Abraham as our father.' I tell you that out of these stones God can raise up children for Abraham. " (Matthew 3:8-9)

A 17. According to Matthew, chapter 3, verse 3, where is a voice calling?
A. In the wilderness (Matthew 3:3)

G 18. Who can raise up children for Abraham out of these stones?
A. God (Matthew 3:9)

G 19. To whom did people go out from Jerusalem and all Judea and the whole region of the Jordan?
A. John (the Baptist) (Matthew 3:5)

G 20. For whom can God raise up children out of these stones?
A. Abraham (Matthew 3:9)
