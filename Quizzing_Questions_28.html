Matthew 28

SET #1

G 1. What did some of the guards report to the chief priests?
A. Everything that had happened (Matthew 28:11)

G 2. To whom did Jesus say, "Do not be afraid. Go and tell my brothers to go to Galilee; there they will see me."?
A. The women [OR Mary Magdalene and the other Mary] (Matthew 28:10)

R 3. Finish these verses and give the reference: "Therefore go and make disciples. . ."
A. ". . .of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age." " (Matthew 28:19-20)

G 4. Like what was the angel's appearance?
A. Lightning (Matthew 28:3)

S 5. Situation question: who said it, and to whom: "Do not be afraid, for I know that you are looking for Jesus, who was crucified."?
A. The angel said it to the women [OR Mary Magdalene and the other Mary] (Matthew 28:5)

G 6. When did the chief priests give the soldiers a large sum of money?
A. When the chief priests had met with the elders and devised a plan (Matthew 28:12)

A 7. According to Matthew, chapter 28, verse 16, where did the eleven disciples go?
A. To Galilee, to the mountain where Jesus had told them to go (Matthew 28:16)

Q 8. Quote Matthew, chapter 28, verse 6.
A. "He is not here; he has risen, just as he said. Come and see the place where he lay." (Matthew 28:6)

X 9. What false report will satisfy the governor and keep the soldiers out of trouble?
A. That Jesus' disciples came during the night and stole Jesus away while the soldiers were asleep (Matthew 28:13-14)

A 10. According to Matthew, chapter 28, verse 19, how should you baptize them?
A. In the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit (Matthew 28:19)

G 11. Who hurried away from the tomb, afraid yet filled with joy?
A. The women [OR Mary Magdalene and the other Mary] (Matthew 28:8)

G 12. Who should go quickly and tell Jesus' disciples: "He has risen from the dead and is going ahead of you into Galilee. There you will see him."?
A. The women [OR Mary Magdalene and the other Mary] (Matthew 28:7)

V 13. Finish this verse: "Then Jesus came to them. . ."
A. ". . .and said, "All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me." (Matthew 28:18)

G 14. Who rolled back the stone and sat on it?
A. An angel of the Lord (Matthew 28:2)

G 15. Of whom were the guards so afraid that they shook and became like dead men?
A. The angel (Matthew 28:4)

A 16. According to Matthew, chapter 28, verse 17, when did they worship him?
A. When they saw him (Matthew 28:17)

G 17. When will the chief priests satisfy the governor and keep the soldiers out of trouble?
A. If this report gets to the governor (Matthew 28:14)

G 18. Who went to look at the tomb after the Sabbath, at dawn on the first day of the week?
A. Mary Magdalene and the other Mary (Matthew 28:1)

A 19. According to Matthew, chapter 28, verse 15, who did as they were instructed?
A. The soldiers (Matthew 28:15)

G 20. Who should teach all nations to obey everything Jesus has commanded them?
A. The eleven disciples (Matthew 28:20)

G 13. Who has risen from the dead and is going ahead of his disciples into Galilee?
A. Jesus (Matthew 28:7)

G 14. Where did some of the guards go and report to the chief priests everything that had happened?
A. Into the city (Matthew 28:11)

X 15. What false report will satisfy the governor and keep the soldiers out of trouble?
A. That Jesus' disciples came during the night and stole Jesus away while the soldiers were asleep (Matthew 28:13-14)

G 16. Who rolled back the stone and sat on it?
A. An angel of the Lord (Matthew 28:2)

A 17. According to Matthew, chapter 28, verse 20, when am I surely with you?
A. Always, to the very end of the age (Matthew 28:20)

G 18. Who are looking for Jesus, who was crucified?
A. The women [OR Mary Magdalene and the other Mary] (Matthew 28:5)

A 19. According to Matthew, chapter 28, verse 4, what were the guards?
A. So afraid of him that they shook and became like dead men (Matthew 28:4)

A 20. According to Matthew, chapter 28, verse 17, when did they worship him?
A. When they saw him (Matthew 28:17)
