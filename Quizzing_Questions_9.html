SET #1

G 1. Who need a doctor?
A. The sick (Matthew 9:12)

X 2. From what Old Testament book does the following quotation come: "I desire mercy, not sacrifice."?
A. Hosea (Matthew 9:13)

G 3. Who was teaching in their synagogues?
A. Jesus (Matthew 9:35)

A 4. According to Matthew, chapter 9, verse 22, what has healed you?
A. Your faith (Matthew 9:22)

V 5. Finish these verses: "On hearing this, Jesus said,. . ."
A. ". . ."It is not the healthy who need a doctor, but the sick. But go and learn what this means: 'I desire mercy, not sacrifice.' For I have not come to call the righteous, but sinners." (Matthew 9:12-13)

A 6. According to Matthew, chapter 9, verse 18, who will live?
A. A synagogue leader's daughter (Matthew 9:18)

G 7. Who is not dead but asleep?
A. The girl [OR the synagogue leader's daughter] (Matthew 9:24)

G 8. Who said to themselves, "This fellow is blaspheming!"?
A. Some of the teachers of the law (Matthew 9:3)

Q 9. Quote Matthew, chapter 9, verses 37 through 38.
A. "Then he said to his disciples, "The harvest is plentiful but the workers are few. Ask the Lord of the harvest, therefore, to send out workers into his harvest field."" (Matthew 9:37-38)

G 10. Where do some of the teachers of the law entertain evil thoughts?
A. In their hearts (Matthew 9:4)

A 11. According to Matthew, chapter 9, verse 2, whom did some men bring to Jesus?
A. A paralyzed man (Matthew 9:2)

G 12. Who said, "It is by the prince of demons that he drives out demons."?
A. The Pharisees (Matthew 9:34)

A 13. According to Matthew, chapter 9, verse 30, what did Jesus warn them?
A. "See that no one knows about this." (Matthew 9:30)

G 14. When will the guests of the bridegroom fast?
A. When the bridegroom is taken from them (Matthew 9:15)

G 15. Whom did the crowd praise?
A. God (Matthew 9:8)

R 16. Finish this verse and give the reference: "When he saw the crowds,. . ."
A. ". . .he had compassion on them, because they were harassed and helpless, like sheep without a shepherd. " (Matthew 9:36)

G 17. Who touched the blind men's eyes?
A. Jesus (Matthew 9:29)

G 18. What is plentiful?
A. The harvest (Matthew 9:37)

S 19. Situation question: who said it, to whom, and what was the response: "My daughter has just died. But come and put your hand on her, and she will live."?
A. A synagogue leader said it to Jesus, and Jesus and his disciples got up and went with the synagogue leader (Matthew 9:18-19)

G 20. What will be ruined if people pour new wine into old wineskins?
A. The wineskins (Matthew 9:17)
