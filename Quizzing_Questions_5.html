A. "You shall not commit adultery" (Matthew 5:27)

A. His sun (Matthew 5:45)

A. Your coat (Matthew 5:40)

A. Even one hair (Matthew 5:36)

A. What are you doing more than others? (Matthew 5:47)

A. Your Father in heaven (Matthew 5:16)
X 18. Two-part answer: in Matthew, chapter 5, who will be subject to judgment?
A. 1) Anyone who murders; 2) Anyone who is angry with a brother or sister (Matthew 5:21-22)

A. ". . .the world. A town built on a hill cannot be hidden. Neither do people light a lamp and put it under a bowl. Instead they put it on its stand, and it gives light to everyone in the house. In the same way, let your light shine before others, that they may see your good deeds and glorify your Father in heaven." (Matthew 5:14-16)
