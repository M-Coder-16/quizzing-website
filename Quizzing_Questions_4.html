SET #1

G 1. Who heard that John had been put in prison?
A. Jesus (Matthew 4:12)

V 2. Finish this verse: "Jesus answered, "It is written:. . ."
A. ". . .'Man shall not live on bread alone, but on every word that comes from the mouth of God.'" (Matthew 4:4)

G 3. How did Peter and Andrew leave their nets and follow Jesus?
A. At once (Matthew 4:20)

A 4. According to Matthew, chapter 4, verse 19, what did Jesus say?
A. "Come, follow me, and I will send you out to fish for people." (Matthew 4:19)

G 5. In what area was Capernaum?
A. The area of Zebulun and Naphtali (Matthew 4:13)

G 6. Why was Jesus led by the Spirit into the wilderness?
A. To be tempted by the devil (Matthew 4:1)

A 7. According to Matthew, chapter 4, verse 2, when was he hungry?
A. After fasting forty days and forty nights (Matthew 4:2)

G 8. What has dawned on those living in the land of the shadow of death?
A. A light (Matthew 4:16)

R 9. Finish this verse and give the reference: "Jesus answered him, "It is. . ."
A. ". . .also written: 'Do not put the Lord your God to the test.'" " (Matthew 4:7)

G 10. What were Peter and Andrew casting into the lake?
A. A net (Matthew 4:18)

G 11. Whom did the devil have stand on the highest point of the temple?
A. Jesus (Matthew 4:5)

S 12. Situation question: who said it, to whom, and in reply to what: "It is also written: 'Do not put the Lord your God to the test.'"?
A. Jesus said it to the devil, in reply to, "If you are the Son of God, throw yourself down. For it is written: 'He will command his angels concerning you, and they will lift you up in their hands, so that you will not strike your foot against a stone.'" (Matthew 4:6-7)

A 13. According to Matthew, chapter 4, verse 21, with whom were James and John?
A. Their father Zebedee (Matthew 4:21)

G 14. Whom did the devil show all the kingdoms of the world and their splendor?
A. Jesus (Matthew 4:8)

A 15. According to Matthew, chapter 4, verse 24, whom did people bring to him?
A. All who were ill with various diseases, those suffering severe pain, the demon-possessed, those having seizures, and the paralyzed (Matthew 4:24)

Q 16. Quote Matthew, chapter 4, verses 19 through 20.
A. "Come, follow me," Jesus said, "and I will send you out to fish for people." At once they left their nets and followed him. (Matthew 4:19-20)

X 17. From what Old Testament book does the following quotation come: "Worship the Lord your God, and serve him only."?
A. Deuteronomy (Matthew 4:10)

G 18. Concerning whom will he command his angels?
A. You [the Son of God OR Jesus] (Matthew 4:6)

G 19. Who followed Jesus from Galilee, the Decapolis, Jerusalem, Judea and the region across the Jordan?
A. Large crowds (Matthew 4:25)

G 20. Whom did James and John follow?
A. Jesus (Matthew 4:22)
