SET #1

G 1. To whom do you say, "Let me take the speck out of your eye."?
A. Your brother (Matthew 7:4)

R 2. Finish this verse and give the reference: "So in everything, do to. . ."
A. ". . .others what you would have them do to you, for this sums up the Law and the Prophets. " (Matthew 7:12)

G 3. Who is like a wise man who built his house on the rock?
A. Everyone who hears these words of Jesus and puts them into practice (Matthew 7:24)

G 4. Whom will Jesus tell plainly, "I never knew you. Away from me, you evildoers!"?
A. Many who will say to Jesus on that day, "Lord, Lord, did we not prophesy in your name and in your name drive out demons and in your name perform many miracles?" (Matthew 7:22-23)

A 5. According to Matthew, chapter 7, verse 14, what is small?
A. The gate that leads to life (Matthew 7:14)

X 6. When will you not give your son a snake?
A. If he asks for a fish (Matthew 7:10)

G 7. What will be opened to the one who knocks?
A. The door (Matthew 7:8)

A 8. According to Matthew, chapter 7, verse 15, what are they inwardly?
A. Ferocious wolves (Matthew 7:15)

V 9. Finish these verses: ""Do not judge, or you. . ."
A. ". . .too will be judged. For in the same way you judge others, you will be judged, and with the measure you use, it will be measured to you." (Matthew 7:1-2)

G 10. What fell with a great crash?
A. A foolish man's house (Matthew 7:27)

G 11. What should you not give what is sacred?
A. Dogs (Matthew 7:6)

G 12. Who will say to Jesus on that day, "Lord, Lord, did we not prophesy in your name and in your name drive out demons and in your name perform many miracles?"
A. Many (Matthew 7:22)

A 13. According to Matthew, chapter 7, verse 19, what is thrown into the fire?
A. Every tree that does not bear good fruit (Matthew 7:19)

Q 14. Quote Matthew, chapter 7, verses 7 through 8.
A. "Ask and it will be given to you; seek and you will find; knock and the door will be opened to you. For everyone who asks receives; the one who seeks finds; and to the one who knocks, the door will be opened. (Matthew 7:7-8)

G 15. Where is the speck of sawdust?
A. In your brother's eye (Matthew 7:3)

G 16. How will you recognize false prophets?
A. By their fruit (Matthew 7:16)

A 17. According to Matthew, chapter 7, verse 2, how will you be judged?
A. In the same way you judge others (Matthew 7:2)

G 18. Why were the crowds amazed at Jesus' teaching?
A. Because he taught as one who had authority, and not as their teachers of the law (Matthew 7:28-29)

G 19. What blew and beat against a wise man's house?
A. The winds (Matthew 7:25)
