A. Elijah (Matthew 17:12)

A. Peter (Matthew 17:26)

A. ". . .them Moses and Elijah, talking with Jesus." (Matthew 17:3)

A. When they looked up (Matthew 17:8)

A. Jesus (Matthew 17:17)

A. When they came together in Galilee (Matthew 17:22)

A. Jesus (Matthew 17:18)

A. Jesus' disciples (Matthew 17:16)

A. Two drachmas (Matthew 17:24)

G 18. When did a bright cloud cover them?
A. While Peter was still speaking (Matthew 17:5)

A. A man said it to Jesus (Matthew 17:15)

A. When they came together in Galilee (Matthew 17:22)
