A. Pilate's wife (Matthew 27:19)

A 10. According to Matthew, chapter 27, verse 24, what did Pilate see?
A. That he was getting nowhere, but that instead an uproar was starting (Matthew 27:24)

S 11. Situation question: who said it, and what was the result: "It is against the law to put this into the treasury, since it is blood money."?
A. The chief priests said it, and they decided to use the money to buy the potter's field as a burial place for foreigners (Matthew 27:6-7)

G 12. Who saw that Jesus was condemned?
A. Judas (Matthew 27:3)

G 13. Whom did Pilate hand over to be crucified?
A. Jesus (Matthew 27:26)

Q 14. Quote Matthew, chapter 27, verse 50.
A. "And when Jesus had cried out again in a loud voice, he gave up his spirit." (Matthew 27:50)

G 15. Whom did Pilate release to the crowd?
A. Barabbas (Matthew 27:26)

G 16. When did Judas return the thirty pieces of silver to the chief priests and the elders?
A. When he saw that Jesus was condemned (Matthew 27:3)

A 17. According to Matthew, chapter 27, verse 56, of whom was Mary the mother?
A. James and Joseph (Matthew 27:56)

G 18. Where was Pilate sitting?
A. On the judge's seat (Matthew 27:19)

G 19. Who said, "Hail, king of the Jews!"?
A. The soldiers (Matthew 27:29)

A 20. According to Matthew, chapter 27, verse 14, to what did Jesus not even make a reply?
A. A single charge (Matthew 27:14)
