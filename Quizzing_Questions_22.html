Matthew 22

SET #1

G 1. Whom did Jesus ask, "What do you think about the Messiah? Whose son is he?"
A. The Pharisees (Matthew 22:41-42)

G 2. When did the Sadducees come to Jesus with a question?
A. That same day (Matthew 22:23)

R 3. Finish these verses and give the reference: "Jesus replied: "'Love the Lord. . ."
A. ". . .your God with all your heart and with all your soul and with all your mind.' This is the first and greatest commandment. " (Matthew 22:37-38)

A 4. According to Matthew, chapter 22, verse 30, like whom will people be at the resurrection?
A. The angels in heaven (Matthew 22:30)

G 5. Who dared to ask Jesus any more questions from that day on?
A. No one (Matthew 22:46)

G 6. Whom did the Pharisees lay plans to trap in his words?
A. Jesus (Matthew 22:15)

X 7. In Matthew, chapter 22, what was a special tax levied on subject peoples?
A. The imperial tax (Matthew 22:17)

G 8. Who tested Jesus with this question: "Teacher, which is the greatest commandment in the Law?"
A. One of the Pharisees (an expert in the law) (Matthew 22:35-36)

G 9. Who heard that Jesus had silenced the Sadducees?
A. The Pharisees (Matthew 22:34)

V 10. Finish this verse: "Jesus replied, "You are in. . ."
A. "... error because you do not know the Scriptures or the power of God." (Matthew 22:29)

G 11. How should you love the Lord your God?
A. With all your heart and with all your soul and with all your mind (Matthew 22:37)

A 12. According to Matthew, chapter 22, verse 9, where should they go?
A. To the street corners (Matthew 22:9)

G 13. Who sent some more servants and said, "Tell those who have been invited that I have prepared my dinner: My oxen and fattened cattle have been butchered, and everything is ready. Come to the wedding banquet."?
A. A king (Matthew 22:4)

G 14. Who is not the God of the dead but of the living?
A. The God of Abraham, Isaac and Jacob (Matthew 22:32)

A 15. According to Matthew, chapter 22, verse 25, who had no children?
A. The first brother (Matthew 22:25)

S 16. Situation question: who said it, to whom, and what was the response: "How did you get in here without wedding clothes, friend?"
A. The king said it to the man not wearing wedding clothes, and the man was speechless (Matthew 22:11-12)

A 17. According to Matthew, chapter 22, verse 22, when were they amazed?
A. When they heard this (Matthew 22:22)

G 18. Who said, "How is it then that David, speaking by the Spirit, calls him 'Lord'?"
A. Jesus (Matthew 22:43)

Q 19. Quote Matthew, chapter 22, verses 31 through 32.
A. "But about the resurrection of the dead - have you not read what God said to you, 'I am the God of Abraham, the God of Isaac, and the God of Jacob'? He is not the God of the dead but of the living."" (Matthew 22:31-32)

G 20. What coin should the Pharisees' disciples and the Herodians show Jesus?
A. The coin used for paying the tax (Matthew 22:19)

X 3. In Matthew, chapter 22, what was a special tax levied on subject peoples?
A. The imperial tax (Matthew 22:17)

G 4. What should you give back to God?
A. What is God's (Matthew 22:21)

G 5. Who prepared a wedding banquet for his son?
A. A king (Matthew 22:2)

Q 6. Quote Matthew, chapter 22, verses 39 through 40.
A. "And the second is like it: 'Love your neighbor as yourself.' All the Law and the Prophets hang on these two commandments."" (Matthew 22:39-40)

A 7. According to Matthew, chapter 22, verse 43, what does David call him?
A. "Lord" (Matthew 22:43)

G 8. Who told the attendants, "Tie him hand and foot, and throw him outside, into the darkness, where there will be weeping and gnashing of teeth."?
A. The king (Matthew 22:13)

G 9. What coin should the Pharisees' disciples and the Herodians show Jesus?
A. The coin used for paying the tax (Matthew 22:19)

R 10. Finish these verses and give the reference: "But about the resurrection of. . ."
A. ". . .the dead - have you not read what God said to you, 'I am the God of Abraham, the God of Isaac, and the God of Jacob'? He is not the God of the dead but of the living." " (Matthew 22:31-32)

G 11. To what does Jesus pay no attention?
A. Who others are (Matthew 22:16)

G 12. What does Jesus teach in accordance with the truth?
A. The way of God (Matthew 22:16)

V 13. Finish this verse: "Jesus replied, "You are in. . ."
A. "... error because you do not know the Scriptures or the power of God." (Matthew 22:29)

G 14. What was filled with guests?
A. The wedding hall (Matthew 22:10)

S 15. Situation question: who said it, to whom, and what was the reply: "Whose image is this? And whose inscription?"
A. Jesus said it to the Pharisees' disciples and the Herodians, and the reply was, "Caesar's." (Matthew 22:20-21)

G 16. With what did the Sadducees come to Jesus?
A. A question (Matthew 22:23)

G 17. Whom did the Pharisees lay plans to trap in his words?
A. Jesus (Matthew 22:15)

A 18. According to Matthew, chapter 22, verse 44, where will I put your enemies?
A. Under your feet (Matthew 22:44)

G 19. What did a king prepare for his son?
A. A wedding banquet (Matthew 22:2)

A 20. According to Matthew, chapter 22, verse 5, where did one go?
A. To his field (Matthew 22:5)
