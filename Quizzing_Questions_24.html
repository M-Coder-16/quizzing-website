Matthew 24

SET #1

G 1. Who will come in Jesus' name, claiming, "I am the Messiah"?
A. Many (Matthew 24:5)

A 2. According to Matthew, chapter 24, verse 19, what will it be in those days?
A. Dreadful for pregnant women and nursing mothers (Matthew 24:19)

G 3. Who will be left in the field?
A. The other man (Matthew 24:40)

R 4. Finish this verse and give the reference: ""Then will appear the sign. . ."
A. ". . .of the Son of Man in heaven. And then all the peoples of the earth will mourn when they see the Son of Man coming on the clouds of heaven, with power and great glory. " (Matthew 24:30)

A 5. According to Matthew, chapter 24, verse 32, what should you learn?
A. This lesson from the fig tree: As soon as its twigs get tender and its leaves come out, you know that summer is near (Matthew 24:32)

S 6. Situation question: who said it, and to whom: "Watch out that no one deceives you."?
A. Jesus said it to the disciples (Matthew 24:4)

G 7. When were people marrying and giving in marriage, up to the day Noah entered the ark?
A. In the days before the flood (Matthew 24:38)

G 8. Where will you see the abomination that causes desolation?
A. Standing in the holy place (Matthew 24:15)

V 9. Finish this verse: ""But about that day or. . ."
A. ". . .hour no one knows, not even the angels in heaven, nor the Son, but only the Father." (Matthew 24:36)

X 10. In Matthew, chapter 24, what will increase?
A. Wickedness (Matthew 24:12)

A 11. According to Matthew, chapter 24, verse 26, when should you not believe it?
A. If anyone tells you, "Here he is, in the inner rooms" (Matthew 24:26)

G 12. Who will betray and hate each other at that time?
A. Many (Matthew 24:10)

G 13. Who will appear and perform great signs and wonders to deceive, if possible, even the elect?
A. False messiahs and false prophets (Matthew 24:24)

Q 14. Quote Matthew, chapter 24, verse 35.
A. "Heaven and earth will pass away, but my words will never pass away." (Matthew 24:35)

G 15. Whom will the master assign a place with the hypocrites?
A. That servant (Matthew 24:51)

G 16. What rumors will you hear?
A. Rumors of wars (Matthew 24:6)

A 17. According to Matthew, chapter 24, verse 29, what will be shaken?
A. The heavenly bodies (Matthew 24:29)

G 18. Who came up to Jesus to call his attention to the temple's buildings?
A. His disciples (Matthew 24:1)

G 19. Who would have kept watch and would not have let his house be broken into if he knew at what time of night the thief was coming?
A. The owner of the house (Matthew 24:43)

G 20. To whom will this gospel of the kingdom be preached in the whole world as a testimony?
A. All nations (Matthew 24:14)

A 17. According to Matthew, chapter 24, verse 41, what will two women be doing?
A. Grinding with a hand mill (Matthew 24:41)

G 18. What will be shortened for the sake of the elect?
A. Those days (Matthew 24:22)

G 19. Who knows about that day or hour?
A. Only the Father (no one else, not even the angels in heaven, nor the Son) (Matthew 24:36)

G 20. About what did people know nothing until the flood came and took them all away?
A. What would happen (Matthew 24:39)
