Matthew 19

SET #1

G 1. Who will judge the twelve tribes of Israel?
A. The disciples who have followed Jesus (Matthew 19:28)

G 2. Whom did Peter answer, "We have left everything to follow you! What then will there be for us?"
A. Jesus (Matthew 19:27)

R 3. Finish this verse and give the reference: "But many who are first. . ."
A. ". . .will be last, and many who are last will be first." (Matthew 19:30)

S 4. Situation question: who said it, to whom, and in reply to what: "Which ones?"
A. A man said it to Jesus, in reply to, "Why do you ask me about what is good? There is only One who is good. If you want to enter life, keep the commandments." (Matthew 19:17-18)

G 5. Who will become one flesh?
A. The two [OR a man and his wife] (Matthew 19:5)

G 6. Who made them male and female at the beginning?
A. The Creator (Matthew 19:4)

X 7. Complete, in essence, the following Old Testament quotation: "You shall not murder, you . . ."
A. ". . . shall not commit adultery, you shall not steal, you shall not give false testimony, honor your father and mother." (Matthew 19:18-19)

G 8. What does anyone who divorces his wife, except for sexual immorality, and marries another woman commit?
A. Adultery (Matthew 19:9)

Q 9. Quote Matthew, chapter 19, verse 26.
A. "Jesus looked at them and said, "With man this is impossible, but with God all things are possible."" (Matthew 19:26)

A 10. According to Matthew, chapter 19, verse 13, whom did people bring to Jesus?
A. Little children (Matthew 19:13)

G 11. What if the young man wants to be perfect?
A. He should go, sell his possessions and give to the poor (and he will have treasure in heaven). Then come, follow Jesus (Matthew 19:21)

G 12. Why should the disciples let the little children come to Jesus and not hinder them?
A. For the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these (Matthew 19:14)

A 13. According to Matthew, chapter 19, verse 19, whom should you honor?
A. Your father and mother (Matthew 19:19)

G 14. Who came up to Jesus and asked, "Teacher, what good thing must I do to get eternal life?"
A. A man (Matthew 19:16)

A 15. According to Matthew, chapter 19, verse 22, what did the young man have?
A. Great wealth (Matthew 19:22)

V 16. Finish this verse: "Again I tell you, it. . ."
A. ". . .is easier for a camel to go through the eye of a needle than for someone who is rich to enter the kingdom of God." (Matthew 19:24)

G 17. Who said to his disciples, "Truly I tell you, it is hard for someone who is rich to enter the kingdom of heaven."?
A. Jesus (Matthew 19:23)

G 18. Who replied, "Why do you ask me about what is good? There is only One who is good. If you want to enter life, keep the commandments."?
A. Jesus (Matthew 19:17)

A 19. According to Matthew, chapter 19, verse 7, what did they ask?
A. "Why then did Moses command that a man give his wife a certificate of divorce and send her away?" (Matthew 19:7)

G 20. What can only those to whom it has been given accept?
A. This word ("If this is the situation between a husband and wife, it is better not to marry.") (Matthew 19:11)

A. ". . .said, "With man this is impossible, but with God all things are possible." (Matthew 19:26)

X 12. Complete, in essence, the following Old Testament quotation: "You shall not murder, you . . ."
A. ". . . shall not commit adultery, you shall not steal, you shall not give false testimony, honor your father and mother." (Matthew 19:18-19)

A 13. According to Matthew, chapter 19, verse 22, what did the young man have?
A. Great wealth (Matthew 19:22)

G 14. To whom did people bring little children?
A. Jesus (Matthew 19:13)

A 15. According to Matthew, chapter 19, verse 19, how should you love your neighbor?
A. As yourself (Matthew 19:19)

G 16. What will a man and his wife become?
A. One flesh (Matthew 19:5)

G 17. What can only those to whom it has been given accept?
A. This word ("If this is the situation between a husband and wife, it is better not to marry.") (Matthew 19:11)

A 18. According to Matthew, chapter 19, verse 2, whom did he heal there?
A. Large crowds (Matthew 19:2)

G 19. Who said to his disciples, "Truly I tell you, it is hard for someone who is rich to enter the kingdom of heaven."?
A. Jesus (Matthew 19:23)

G 20. Why should the disciples let the little children come to Jesus and not hinder them?
A. For the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these (Matthew 19:14)
