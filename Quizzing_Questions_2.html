G 1. When did the Magi open their treasures and present the child with gifts of gold, frankincense and myrrh?
A. After they bowed down and worshiped him (Matthew 2:11)

G 2. Who was born in Bethlehem in Judea, during the time of King Herod?
A. Jesus (Matthew 2:1)

S 3. Situation question: who said it, and to whom: "Get up, take the child and his mother and escape to Egypt. Stay there until I tell you, for Herod is going to search for the child to kill him."?
A. An angel of the Lord said it to Joseph (Matthew 2:13)

G 4. Who worshiped the child?
A. The Magi (Matthew 2:11)

Q 5. Quote Matthew, chapter 2, verses 1 through 2.
A. "After Jesus was born in Bethlehem in Judea, during the time of King Herod, Magi from the east came to Jerusalem and asked, "Where is the one who has been born king of the Jews? We saw his star when it rose and have come to worship him."" (Matthew 2:1-2)

G 6. In place of whom was Archelaus reigning in Judea?
A. His father Herod (Matthew 2:22)

G 7. When did the Magi see the child with his mother Mary?
A. On coming to the house (Matthew 2:11)

R 8. Finish this verse and give the reference: "On coming to the house,. . ."
A. ". . .they saw the child with his mother Mary, and they bowed down and worshiped him. Then they opened their treasures and presented him with gifts of gold, frankincense and myrrh. " (Matthew 2:11)

A 9. According to Matthew, chapter 2, verse 10, what did they see?
A. The star (Matthew 2:10)

G 10. What had the Magi been warned in a dream?
A. Not to go back to Herod (Matthew 2:12)

G 11. Whom did the Magi see on coming to the house?
A. The child with his mother Mary (Matthew 2:11)
