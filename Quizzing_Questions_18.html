SET #1

A. His fellow servant (Matthew 18:29)

A. If it causes you to stumble (Matthew 18:9)

A. My Father in heaven (Matthew 18:19)

A. Woe (Matthew 18:7)

A. Their angels in heaven always see the face of Jesus' Father in heaven (Matthew 18:10)

A. A king who wanted to settle accounts with his servants (Matthew 18:23)

A. "And he said: "Truly I tell you, unless you change and become like little children, you will never enter the kingdom of heaven." (Matthew 18:3)

A. To be tortured until he should pay back all he owed (Matthew 18:34)

G 11. Who is there with them when two or three gather in Jesus' name?
A. Jesus (Matthew 18:20)

X 12. Complete, in essence, the following: "He called a little child . . ."
A. ". . . to him, and placed the child among them." (Matthew 18:2)

A 13. According to Matthew, chapter 18, verse 25, what was he not able to do?
A. Pay (Matthew 18:25)

G 14. Who were outraged?
A. The other servants (Matthew 18:31)

A 15. According to Matthew, chapter 18, verse 28, what did one of his fellow servants owe that servant?
A. A hundred silver coins (Matthew 18:28)

V 16. Finish this verse: "And he said: "Truly I. . ."
A. ". . .tell you, unless you change and become like little children, you will never enter the kingdom of heaven." (Matthew 18:3)

G 17. Who is the greatest in the kingdom of heaven?
A. Whoever takes the lowly position of this child (Matthew 18:4)

G 18. What will whatever you bind on earth be?
A. Bound in heaven (Matthew 18:18)

S 19. Situation question: who said it, to whom, and what was the reply: "Lord, how many times shall I forgive my brother or sister who sins against me? Up to seven times?"
A. Peter said it to Jesus, and the reply was, "I tell you, not seven times, but seventy-seven times." (Matthew 18:21-22)

G 20. Whom should you treat as you would a pagan or a tax collector if they refuse to listen even to the church?

G 3. When would it be better for them to have a large millstone hung around their neck and to be drowned in the depths of the sea?
A. If anyone causes one of these little ones - those who believe in Jesus - to stumble (Matthew 18:6)

G 4. Who were outraged?
A. The other servants (Matthew 18:31)

Q 5. Quote Matthew, chapter 18, verses 19 through 20.
A. "Again, truly I tell you that if two of you on earth agree about anything they ask for, it will be done for them by my Father in heaven. For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them." (Matthew 18:19-20)

A 6. According to Matthew, chapter 18, verse 23, like what is the kingdom of heaven?
A. A king who wanted to settle accounts with his servants (Matthew 18:23)

G 7. Until when did his master hand the servant over to the jailers to be tortured?
A. Until he should pay back all he owed (Matthew 18:34)

A 8. According to Matthew, chapter 18, verse 25, what was he not able to do?
A. Pay (Matthew 18:25)

G 9. Who took pity on the servant?
A. The servant's master (Matthew 18:27)

G 10. What may be established by the testimony of two or three witnesses?
A. Every matter (Matthew 18:16)

R 11. Finish these verses and give the reference: ""Again, truly I tell you. . ."
A. ". . .that if two of you on earth agree about anything they ask for, it will be done for them by my Father in heaven. For where two or three gather in my name, there am I with them." " (Matthew 18:19-20)

G 12. Who begged that servant, "Be patient with me, and I will pay it back."?
A. His fellow servant (Matthew 18:29)

G 13. Than what is it better for you to enter life with one eye?
A. Than to have two eyes and be thrown into the fire of hell (Matthew 18:9)

V 14. Finish this verse: "And he said: "Truly I. . ."
A. ". . .tell you, unless you change and become like little children, you will never enter the kingdom of heaven." (Matthew 18:3)

X 15. Complete, in essence, the following: "He called a little child . . ."
A. ". . . to him, and placed the child among them." (Matthew 18:2)

G 16. What should you point out, just between the two of you, if your brother or sister sins?
A. Their fault (Matthew 18:15)

G 17. Why should you not despise one of these little ones?
A. Their angels in heaven always see the face of Jesus' Father in heaven (Matthew 18:10)

A 18. According to Matthew, chapter 18, verse 17, how should you treat them if they refuse to listen even to the church?
A. As you would a pagan or a tax collector (Matthew 18:17)

G 19. Who answered, "I tell you, not seven times, but seventy-seven times."?
A. Jesus (Matthew 18:22)

G 20. What if a man finds the one sheep that wandered off?
A. He is happier about that one sheep than about the ninety-nine that did not wander off (Matthew 18:13)
